Manali, Himachal Pradhesh

Nestled in the picturesque Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, Manali is a serene haven that captivates travelers with its breathtaking landscapes and vibrant culture. The town, situated at an altitude of 6,726 feet, offers an enchanting blend of snow-capped mountains, lush valleys, and gushing rivers, making it a year-round destination. Manali boasts a plethora of attractions that cater to various interests. Rohtang Pass, a high mountain pass, offers panoramic views of the surrounding peaks and is a gateway to Lahaul-Spiti Valley. Solang Valley is a hub for adventure enthusiasts, offering activities like paragliding, zorbing, and skiing during winters. The Beas River snakes through the valley, providing opportunities for river rafting and serene riverside strolls.
